Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep it simple and we don't sell your data to anyone.

Who we are

The Wild Learning Collective is a sole trader business operated by Matthias Clarke. Our website is at wildlearningcollective.com and you can reach us at hello@wildlearningcollective.com.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, Matthias Clarke (trading as The Wild Learning Collective) is the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

What information we collect

When you buy something — name, email address, delivery address, and payment details. Payment is processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. Stripe's own privacy policy applies to how they handle your payment information.

When you sign up for our newsletter or discount offer — your email address only. We use this to send you offers, new book announcements, and news about the Wild Learning Collective. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

When you send a school enquiry — your name, email address, school name, and any other details you choose to include. We use this only to respond to your enquiry.

When you visit our website — we do not currently use tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent where required.

How we use your information

  • To process and fulfil your order
  • To send you order confirmation and delivery updates
  • To send marketing emails if you have opted in (you can opt out at any time)
  • To respond to school and business enquiries
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your information for any automated decision-making or profiling.

Who we share your data with

We share your information only where necessary to run the business:

  • Stripe — payment processing. See stripe.com/gb/privacy.
  • Royal Mail / courier services — your name and delivery address, to fulfil your order.
  • Email marketing provider — your email address, if you have signed up for our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any other third party.

How long we keep your data

Order and customer records are kept for six years in line with HMRC requirements. Marketing email subscriptions are held until you unsubscribe. School enquiry emails are kept for up to twelve months unless we are in an active relationship with your school.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information (where we are not legally required to keep it)
  • Object to or restrict how we use your information
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@wildlearningcollective.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

Our website does not currently use cookies for tracking or analytics. Stripe may set cookies during the checkout process as part of their fraud prevention systems. We do not have access to or control over those cookies.

Children's privacy

Our products are for children but our website and purchasing process is directed at adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

For our Wild Challenge, families may share photos of nature and activities with us. We never publish images of children on our public website. Any community sharing is opt-in and clearly explained at the point of submission.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. We will notify subscribers of any significant changes by email.
